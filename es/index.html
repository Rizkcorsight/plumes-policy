Política de privacidad

Plumes is an offline feather atlas and journal. It has no account, no ads, no analytics, and stores your journal entries on your device.

What the app stores

The app stores the information you create or choose to save in the app on your device. This may include settings, notes, photos, documents, journal entries, study material, or other app-specific content.

What we do not collect

The app does not require an account and does not collect your name, email address, phone number, advertising identifiers, contacts, or usage analytics for the developer.

Analytics, ads, and tracking

The app does not include third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or cross-app tracking SDKs.

Network use

The app is designed to work locally or offline where possible. If you choose to open a link, use a store purchase, fetch public reference data, or use a map or government data service, that request is handled by the relevant service and its own privacy policy.

Sharing and exports

If you export, share, or back up app content, you choose the destination. That destination may have its own privacy practices.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
